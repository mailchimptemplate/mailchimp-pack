Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Lead Generation feature work?

You can choose to lock the entire flipbook or specific pages. Visitors will see a popup asking for their email address to continue reading. Once submitted, the leads are saved in your WordPress database.

Can I sell products directly from the flipbook?

Yes! Our Shoppable Hotspots feature allows you to add clickable links, tags, and product popups directly over any image or PDF page. You can easily link them to your WooCommerce checkout or product pages.
