Frequently Asked Questions

Can I assign a specific chart to a specific product?

Yes! You can assign a size guide globally, assign it to a specific WooCommerce Category (e.g., "Men's Shirts"), or select individual products where the guide should appear.

Can I add measurement instruction images?

Absolutely. Along with the data table, you can upload an image (e.g., a diagram showing how to measure chest, waist, etc.) that will appear inside the popup alongside the size chart.

Where does the 'Size Guide' button appear?

By default, the plugin neatly integrates the button near the "Add to Cart" or "Variations" section of the WooCommerce single product page. You can also place it anywhere using a shortcode.
