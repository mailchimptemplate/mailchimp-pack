Frequently Asked Questions

Will this completely prevent lawsuits?

While no automated tool can guarantee 100% legal immunity, installing an accessibility widget immediately demonstrates "good faith" compliance. It deters 99% of automated legal trolls who scan the internet looking for easy targets without any accessibility features.

How is this different from AccessiBe or UserWay?

Those platforms are SaaS products that inject scripts from their servers and charge you $490+ every year. Our plugin provides the exact same front-end interface features but is self-hosted on your WordPress site, and you only pay $29 once.

Does it affect website loading speed?

No. We have built-in a "Core Web Vitals Optimizer" performance mode. When enabled, the plugin lazyloads all Javascript files only when a user interacts (scrolls, touches, or moves the mouse), keeping your PageSpeed score at 100/100.

How does the AI Alt-Text Generator work?

The plugin scans your Media Library and parses images that lack alternative text. Using our AI engine integration, it analyzes the graphical content and outputs clean, contextual descriptions directly to the WP Database, making them instantly readable by screen-readers.

Does it support WooCommerce checkout and product pages?

Yes. Our dedicated WooCommerce Fixer scans dynamically generated cart elements and product pages. It automatically appends descriptive ARIA labels to close icons, quantitator plus/minus buttons, and assigns alert roles to system error notices.
