How to Create Your First Flipbook

Turn any PDF or image gallery into a realistic 3D flipbook in seconds.

1 Add a New Book

Go to your WordPress Dashboard. In the left menu, click on FlipBook Studio > Add New Book. Enter a title for your flipbook at the top of the page.

2 Upload PDF or Images

Under the Source tab, click the Browse button. Select a PDF file from your Media Library.

Tip: You can also select multiple images by holding the SHIFT key to create an image-based flipbook!

3 Save and Publish

Click the blue Publish / Save FlipBook button at the top right. Once saved, copy the generated shortcode (e.g., [flipbook id="123"]) and paste it into any post or page.

Setting a Custom Cover Image

Replace the default black cover with a high-quality image of your choice.

Note: If your PDF is in Landscape mode (wide), the first page is automatically split into two. Using a custom Portrait (tall) cover image fixes the layout instantly.

1 Enable Custom Cover

Go to the Source tab and toggle the Custom Cover switch to ON.

2 Upload Cover Image

Click the Upload button and select an image (preferably a vertical/portrait image like a book cover). Save the flipbook, and your new cover will appear immediately on the live site.

Adding Shoppable Hotspots

Make your catalogs interactive by adding "Buy Now" tags directly over product images.

1 Open the Visual Editor

Click on the Interactive Studio tab. You will see a live preview of your book. Use the Next/Prev arrows to navigate to the page where you want to add the hotspot.

2 Place the Pin

Click the red Add Hotspot button at the top. Then, click anywhere on the book page to drop the pin. You can drag the pin around to adjust its exact location.

3 Configure Product Details

A configuration form will appear. Enter the Product Title, Price, upload a Product Image, and paste the Link to your checkout/product page. Click Save Changes.

Embedding Video & Audio

Bring pages to life by embedding YouTube, Vimeo, MP4, MP3, or Google Maps.

1 Select Add Media

In the Interactive Studio tab, go to your desired page. Click the green Add Media button and click on the page to drop the pin.

2 Choose Media Type

In the configuration form, select the type of media from the dropdown menu (e.g., YouTube Video, Self-Hosted MP4, Audio MP3, Google Map).

3 Set Size & URL

Paste the URL of your media (e.g., `https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=...`). Adjust the Width (%) and Height (%) sliders to fit the video perfectly on your page. Click Save Changes.

Setting Up Lead Generation (Gate Lock)

Force users to submit their Name & Email before they can read the rest of the book.

How it works: The user reads a few pages for free. Suddenly, a beautiful blur overlay appears asking for their email. Once submitted, the book unlocks instantly!

1 Enable Content Lock

Go to the Content Lock tab and turn the switch ON.

2 Set the Lock Page

Enter the page number where the lock should appear (e.g., Page `4`).

3 Connect Google Sheets (Optional)

You can paste your Google Apps Script URL here to automatically send the collected emails to your spreadsheet. Leave it blank if you just want a demo/dummy unlock.

Custom Branding & Colors

Make the flipbook match your website's exact theme and brand identity.

1 Open Appearance Tab

Go to the Appearance tab and toggle the Custom Branding Colors switch ON.

2 Change Colors

Use the visual color pickers to change the Book Background, Control Bar background, Icon colors, and Hotspot Pulse colors.

3 Pulse Animation

You can turn ON or OFF the radar-like pulse animation that surrounds the shoppable hotspots from this tab.
