Frequently Asked Questions

Are there any monthly subscription fees?

No. We strongly believe in owning your tools. You pay $29 once and get lifetime access to the plugin and its updates for the major version.

Do I need the official WhatsApp Business API?

No API is required! The plugin simply redirects users to the native WhatsApp app (mobile or web) with a pre-filled message. Your agents can use their standard WhatsApp or WhatsApp Business app.

Where are the captured leads stored?

Unlike SaaS platforms that hold your data hostage, all leads captured by our form are securely saved directly into your own WordPress database. You can view or export them anytime from the WP Admin dashboard.

Will this slow down my website?

Not at all. The frontend is built with pure Vanilla JavaScript (No jQuery) and weighs just a few kilobytes. It's fully optimized for Core Web Vitals.
