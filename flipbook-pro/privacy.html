Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 23, 2026

Welcome to ChimpJar FlipBook Pro. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your information when you use our website and software-as-a-service (SaaS) platform.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our service, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and password when you register for an account.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our editor, how many flipbooks you create, and the analytics (views) of your published books.
  • Lead Generation Data: If you use our LeadGen feature, the names and emails submitted by your readers are sent directly to your configured Google Sheets. We do not store or process your readers' email addresses on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and maintain our services.
  • To manage your account and AppSumo lifetime deal status.
  • To improve, personalize, and expand our platform.
  • To communicate with you, including customer support and updates.

3. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services to facilitate our platform, primarily Google Firebase (for database and authentication). Your data is securely stored on Firebase servers in compliance with their strict privacy policies.

4. Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please visit our Contact Support page.
